MMLA Workshop

This workshop on Interaction Analysis and Learning Analytics will take place on May 2-4, 2019 on Northwestern University's Evanston Campus. This workshop is supported by NSF Award #1832234 - Catalyzing Research in Multi-modal Learning Analytics.

All of your travel, hotel and food expenses will be paid for through the NSF grant.

    Goals:
  1. identify, discuss and develop opportunities to utilize computational techniques and/or multimodal data in learning sciences and
  2. increase awareness of these tools, their best practices, and potential challenges to their use, within the larger learning sciences community. Furthermore, we hope to establish new collaborations among researchers from different institutions, and develop a set of best practices for using computational techniques and multimodal data to support interaction analysis.

If you have any questions, please contact Marcelo Worsley or Sarah Lee.
